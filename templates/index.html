※ Introduction

Here, we constructed a well-curated database of PTM regulation on liquid-liquid Phase Separation (PTMPhaSe) that contains manually curated complete experimental evidence of PTMs modulating LLPS (promotion and inhibition) in 14 organisms, mainly including H. sapines, M. musculus, C. elegans, S. cerevisiae, R. norvegicus and A. thaliana. Currently, PTMPhaSe V1.0 encompasses 733 non-redundant PTM sites of 164 proteins that modulating LLPS involving 82 diseases. Various annotations were systematically integrated, such as sequence properties, structure properties and protein-protein interactions(PPI). We believed that PTMPhaSe could serve as a powerful platform to gain a better understanding of disease development, disease diagnosis and drug discovery.
